Welcome

Find friends for life and life in Christ with Stanford Christian Students! We are a warm and loving community where you can grow spiritually, develop a personal relationship with Jesus, and receive mentoring and shepherding. We love to read the Bible, seek to understand the Word in a deeper way, and practice to apply it to our daily life. Come and see (John 1:46)!

Bible Studies

We meet in groups to read and enjoy the Word of God every week. Our main focus in these times is to make the Word applicable to our Christian walk.

Worship Service

Every Sunday morning, we gather together for worship and a short sermon. In addition, we have an open mic time for anyone to share a testimony from their walk with Christ!

Companionship​/​Mentorship

SCS strives to provide a community where you'll always have a friend to pursue God and His Word. The staff and students love to gather in 2's and 3's (Matthew 18:20) to grow our relationships with Jesus Christ and one another.

Small Groups

We visit families in the surrounding cities for dinner and fellowship. Come enjoy a home cooked meal, singing, and mutual shepherding.

Fall/Spring Retreats

Every semester, we join other Christian clubs in the Bay Area for a weekend of singing, rejoicing, testimonies and speaking from the word of God. Recently, we’ve covered God’s complete salvation and five keys to a Godly living.
