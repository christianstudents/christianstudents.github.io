Welcome

Stanford Christian Students is a community that welcomes all students to read the word of God, to grow in Christ, and to pursue fellowship with us on campus. For our events, we hold weekly Bible studies, host on-campus worship services, and enjoy group gatherings together.

Bible Studies

Meet in groups to read and enjoy the Word of God every week. We don‘t just focus on obtaining doctrinal knowledge. We also emphasize applying the Word of God experientially in our Christian walk.

Worship Service

Every Sunday morning, we gather together for worship and a short sermon. In addition, we have an open mic time for anyone to share a testimony from their walk with Christ!

Meetups

Companionship​/​mentorship: meet and follow after Christ together with students​/​staff in 2’s and 3’s.

Small Groups

We visit families in the surrounding cities for dinner and fellowship. Come enjoy a home cooked meal, singing, and mutual shepherding.

Fall/Spring Retreats

Every fall and spring we join other Christian clubs in the Bay Area for a weekend of singing, rejoicing, testimonies and speaking from the word of God. Past topics include: God’s complete salvation and five keys to a Godly living.
