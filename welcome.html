Welcome!

To the incoming class of 2024: Welcome! Both to Stanford, and to what will surely be some of the most memorable years of your life. For many of us, our college years have afforded us a new beginning in our Christian lives, and as a club we pray that you, too, would be afforded the same opportunity to begin anew with God. Stanford Christian Students welcomes all students, regardless of denominational background, who want to grow in their relationship with the Lord during their college years. 2 Tim. 2:22 says, "Flee youthful lusts, and pursue righteousness, faith, love, peace with those who call on the Lord out of a pure heart." The Christian life is not an easy one, especially by yourself, so we hope to provide a place where you can be connected with other believers and be encouraged to run this Christian race together.
